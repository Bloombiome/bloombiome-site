Harnessing the skin microbiome for timeless beauty

BloomBiome is building the future of skin care through the power of synthetic biology. We engineer live skin-native microbes to promote collagen synthesis, reduce UV damage, and reverse signs of aging.

Technology

We are developing genetically engineered Staphylococcus epidermidis strains capable of delivering bioactive peptides directly to the skin. These strains respond to environmental triggers such as UV exposure to release protective or rejuvenating peptides that support healthy, youthful skin.
